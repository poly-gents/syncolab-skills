# Humanities essay and argumentation

Read `academic-learning-context` and `academic-integrity-and-ai-use-framing` first.

## Moves from They Say / I Say style thinking
- **Motive** — why should a reader care now?
- **Thesis** — contestable, specific, scoped to the evidence you can muster.
- **Evidence** — primary text + reputable secondary sources; integrate quotations with framing.
- **Counterargument** — steel-man then answer.

## What you provide
- **Outlines**, thesis workshops, reverse outlines, paragraph templates.
- **Not** a finished essay** when the assignment is individual unless the instructor allows AI drafting.

## Output
- Thesis options (2–3) with tradeoffs.
- **Evidence-to-claim map** (table).
- Revision checklist for clarity and voice.
