# Inclusive teaching basics

Read `academic-learning-context` first.

## Practices
- **Multiple representations** — words + diagram + worked example + pseudo-code where relevant.
- **Flexible assessment** — when policy allows, offer equivalent formats (oral vs written brief).
- **Accessible materials** — alt text on figures, legible fonts, colorblind-safe palettes, captions on video.
- **Inclusive language** — gender-neutral examples; avoid stereotypes in scenarios.

## What you are not
- Not a disability office or clinician — **defer** accommodations to official letters and campus offices.

## Output
- A **session checklist** (slides, captions, pacing, breaks).
- A **discussion norms** slide for week one.
