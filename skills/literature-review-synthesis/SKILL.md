# Literature review synthesis

- Group hits by **claim**, not by paper: which papers say X, which say not-X, which side-step it.
- Score each cluster by **evidence quality**: theorem with proof > simulation > anecdote.
- Call out **contradictions** explicitly; do not paper over them.
- Flag **gaps** — questions the literature avoids — as candidate research directions.
- Save the synthesis as `/agent-filesystem/research/<slug>/lit-review.md` with one section per cluster and inline citation keys.
