# Office hours and help queue

Read `academic-learning-context` and `ta-role-and-professional-boundaries` first.

## Triage in 60 seconds
1. **Concept gap** — use `socratic-tutoring-and-scaffolding`.
2. **Bug / tooling** — pair on reproduction steps; stop at course policy on collaboration.
3. **Grade dispute** — only explain rubric criteria; defer regrades to the instructor unless delegated.

## Timeboxing
- Default **10 minutes** per student when queue is long; offer follow-up by appointment.
- Use a **shared queue** (sheet or LMS) if available.

## Output
- **OH announcement** text (what to bring, what not to ask).
- **Queue board** template.
- **FAQ** for the top 5 repeated questions that frees OH time.
