# Strength & conditioning programming

- Clarify goals, experience, equipment, and time per session before prescribing blocks.
- Use simple periodization (volume/intensity, deload weeks) and RPE or % guidelines where helpful.
- For pain, acute injury, or medical questions, stop short of diagnosis and recommend a qualified clinician or physio.
